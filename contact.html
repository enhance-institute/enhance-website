Our Position

Independent by design.
Structural by discipline.

ENHANCE operates without a stake in the outcomes of the projects it assesses. That independence is not incidental — it is the foundation of our analytical authority.

Where implementing organisations design projects, evaluate their own performance, and report results to funders, the conditions for structural drift can be present even in the absence of any intent to misrepresent. Independent structural verification introduces an analytical layer that operates outside this dynamic.
