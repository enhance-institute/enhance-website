System Capabilities

What PathFinder-GOV™ delivers

Structural failure in GCF projects is predictable before submission. PathFinder-GOV™ positions your pipeline against the approved GCF portfolio, identifies the structural dimensions most likely to weaken your proposal, and delivers actionable policy guidance — before capital is committed.

01 — Portfolio Positioning

Structural Position Score

Your project pipeline is evaluated across twelve structural dimensions — spanning performance, country risk, and climate vulnerability — and positioned against 303 approved GCF projects via a standardised Z-score. Understand precisely where your pipeline stands before submission.

02 — Country Intelligence

National Vulnerability & Readiness Profile

Integrated scores across six verified indices — ND-GAIN, FSI, GPI, GNI, CPIA, and Readiness — covering 191 countries. Ground your proposals in structured, comparable country-level evidence. Contextualise your pipeline relative to LDC and SIDS peer nations.

03 — Policy Direction

Structural Weakness Identification

PathFinder-GOV™ identifies the three weakest structural dimensions in your project and delivers specific, actionable guidance for each — before they become rejection reasons. The output is calibrated to your country's policy context and GCF portfolio norms.

04 — Diagnostic Output

Structured Diagnostic Report

Each analysis is delivered as a structured diagnostic report — formatted for NDA internal review, Accredited Entity engagement, and pipeline decision-making. A policy-grade output designed for institutional use, not for public disclosure.
