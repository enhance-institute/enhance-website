Services

Structure first.

ENHANCE provides independent structural verification for climate projects and investment portfolios. Our T-C-V framework — ToC:f(x)™, C-FAIR™, and VORTA™ — operates as a single integrated instrument that reveals what conventional evaluation misses: the structural conditions under which a project will survive, and where capital is genuinely warranted.

The Framework

Three models.
One instrument.

T

ToC:f(x)™

Structural coherence verification. Evaluates the causal integrity of the full Theory of Change. Distinguishes designs that look viable from designs that will survive.

C

C-FAIR™

Risk and finance architecture. Identifies physical, institutional, and macro-financial risk factors. Justifies grant structure mathematically. Repositions risk — rather than avoiding it.

V

VORTA™

Impact value quantification. Translates ecological, social, and economic impact into investment language. Supports private capital mobilization and benefit-sharing design.

Individually, each model targets a different dimension of structural integrity. Integrated, they surface the risk-return asymmetry that market consensus systematically fails to price — the analytical basis for identifying structural value in climate investment.
