Services

Diagnosis first.
Design second.

ENHANCE provides two categories of service. Diagnostic services evaluate the structural integrity of existing project architectures. Design services rebuild them — from input to impact — so the causal chain holds before capital is deployed.

Diagnostic Services
Structural Weakness Scan
Rapid structural screening using the ToC:f(x)™ canonical framework. Identifies the specific causal conditions missing from your project architecture. Delivered as a structured report with tier classification and condition-level findings.
Pricing is determined following an initial review of project scope, documentation, and engagement objectives. Contact us to begin the conversation.
Proposal Readiness Review
Full structural evaluation of a concept note or full proposal prior to submission. Covers causal architecture, output-outcome linkages, attribution logic, and structural optimization conditions. Includes diagnostic scorecard and prioritised remediation guidance.
Pricing is determined following an initial review of project scope, documentation, and engagement objectives. Contact us to begin the conversation.
Portfolio Structural Diagnostic
Batch evaluation of a project portfolio using the full ToC:f(x)™ + SaR/ES framework. Produces portfolio-level structural statistics, tier distribution, tail-risk identification, and governance-level recommendations for funders and oversight bodies.
Pricing is determined following an initial review of project scope, documentation, and engagement objectives. Contact us to begin the conversation.
Design Services
Section-Level Design Support
Structural redesign of specific sections within a climate proposal — typically the Theory of Change, results framework, or outcome attribution logic. Applied where the diagnostic identifies concentrated structural weakness.
Pricing is determined following an initial review of project scope, documentation, and engagement objectives. Contact us to begin the conversation.
Structural Reframing Note
Comprehensive reframing of a project's causal logic — from the core constraint definition through output specification, outcome attribution, and impact pathway. Delivered as a structured design brief for integration into the full proposal.
Pricing is determined following an initial review of project scope, documentation, and engagement objectives. Contact us to begin the conversation.
Full CN / FP Structural Redesign
End-to-end structural redesign of a Concept Note or Full Proposal. Includes diagnosis, causal architecture redesign, output-outcome-impact chain formalisation, financial structure alignment (C-FAIR™), and value quantification (VORTA™) where applicable.
Pricing is determined following an initial review of project scope, documentation, and engagement objectives. Contact us to begin the conversation.
